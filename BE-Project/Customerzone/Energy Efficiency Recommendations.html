Energy Efficiency Recommendations

Discover ways to improve energy efficiency and reduce your gas consumption with our expert recommendations:

  • Upgrade to energy-efficient appliances.
  • Ensure proper insulation in your home.
  • Regularly maintain gas appliances.
  • Set thermostats to optimal temperatures.
  • Seal gaps and cracks in your home's structure.
  • Install a programmable thermostat to optimize heating and cooling.
  • Replace or clean air filters regularly for HVAC systems.
  • Use natural light and LED bulbs to reduce electricity consumption.
